    Why drips?
    It would have been very easy for me to craft a clean, slick website fit for a quintessential Brooklyn based designer, but I like creating things that are visually striking and have a touch of my own personality. My aesthetics may not always be ‘beautiful’, but I like to think that they stand out enough to make my work memorable. I come up with a lot of crazy concepts in my spare time, and these drippy drips happen to be one of those things.

    Google Play
    I briefly worked as a design and development intern at Type/Code. During this time, Google contacted us and asked us to create a compelling communication strategy for a certain dynamic new product. I was part of a team that worked extensively on crafting a web-based experience to highlight the exciting new features of this product. Our concept revolved around exploring the semantics of scroll based motion as a tool for delivering a cohesive narrative. My role involved design, strategy and prototype development. Certain details cannot currently be disclosed due to the confidential nature of this project, but there's definitely exciting news to come.
