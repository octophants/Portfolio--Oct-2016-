    Why drips?
    It would have been very easy for me to craft a clean, slick website fit for a quintessential Brooklyn based designer, but I like creating things that are visually striking and have a touch of my own personality. My aesthetics may not always be ‘beautiful’, but I like to think that they stand out enough to make my work memorable. I come up with a lot of crazy concepts in my spare time, and these drippy drips happen to be one of those things.
