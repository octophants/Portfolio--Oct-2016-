    Why drips?
    It would have been very easy for me to craft a clean, slick website fit for a quintessential Brooklyn based designer, but I like creating things that are visually striking and have a touch of my own personality. My aesthetics may not always be ‘beautiful’, but I like to think that they stand out enough to make my work memorable. I come up with a lot of crazy concepts in my spare time, and these drippy drips happen to be one of those things.

    Portal
    Portal is an immersive virtual reality piece which uses the aesthetics of gameplay to create beautiful, liberating, magical experiences. The ride lasts for about 11 minutes and takes you through 10 visually stimulating digital environments, each characterized by a different color and emotional quality. Versions exist for iOS, Oculus Rift and Google Cardboard. The piece was shown at The Kellen Gallery in May. You can check out the old marketing site here. Feel free to contact me for a demo, it's actually pretty fun to fly around trippy worlds.
